It’s not a restaurant, but an experienceWelcome To The Rotlo

A heritage restaurant based out of Vadodara, The Rotlo is an Authentic Gujarati Restaurant, that was set up to serve not only the best Gujarati food, but the best Gujarati experience to all. With heritage interiors and architecture, amazing food and impeccable service, you will forget that you are in a city, once you enter the premises. Right from the moment you enter, to the time you are happily full of our authentic delicious food, you will have an experience to remember.

Rotlo Highlights

Enjoy the most authentic Gujarati experience in Vadodara, by indulging your taste buds in our Unlimited Thali Experience. Cooked with love, served with delight, our Thali is a multi course delight for your taste buds. Right from Farsan, to desserts, with a menu set daily, the Thali is curated to cater to all your savory and sweet cravings, and leave you delightedly full by the end.

Other places have Chefs, at “The Rotlo” we have “Maharaj”. With decades of experience cooking the best Gujarati food, for the elites and royals, our Maharajs curate a new menu everyday. With over 150 dishes to choose from, the menu is derived from seasonal fruits, vegetables and sweets, to create a wholesome experience that leaves you completely satisfied.
